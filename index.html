About Us

Compliance expertise since 1997 — now AI-powered.

ISOXPERT grew out of a multi-disciplinary management & IT consultancy founded in 1997 in Karachi, Pakistan. For more than two decades we've guided manufacturers, exporters and service organisations through certification and compliance — work that demanded precision, deep standards knowledge and a genuine partnership with every client.

That hands-on heritage spans ISO 9001, 14001, 27001 and 22000, social-accountability standards like SA 8000, WRAP and BSCI, supply-chain security frameworks such as C-TPAT and BRC, product certification including CE marking, and systems digitisation with QuickBooks and Sage ACT!.

Today we put that same consulting expertise into an AI-powered platform — so compliance is faster, smarter and audit-ready for every organisation.

ISOXPERT is the digital wing of Company Certification International (CCI) — bringing a global certification and audit body's expertise into modern, AI-powered software.

FAQ

Common questions

What ISOXPERT is, the standards we cover, and how certification works.

What is ISOXPERT?

ISOXPERT is an AI-powered ecosystem of four connected apps — Compliance360, Inspection360, QC360 and Audit360 — for governance, risk, compliance, inspection, quality control and certification audits. It is the digital wing of Company Certification International, an accredited certification body operating since 1997.

Which ISO standards does ISOXPERT support?

ISOXPERT supports the leading ISO management system standards including ISO 9001 (quality), ISO 14001 (environment), ISO 45001 (health & safety), ISO 27001 (information security), ISO 22000 (food safety) and ISO 22301 (business continuity), plus social-accountability, supply-chain and product certification schemes.

Is Company Certification International an accredited certification body?

Yes. Company Certification International (CCI) is a certification body and audit organisation that has issued certificates to companies across manufacturing, trading and services since 1997. ISOXPERT brings that expertise into modern, AI-powered software.

Can ISOXPERT help us get ISO certified?

Yes. CCI provides accredited certification, while the ISOXPERT platform helps you prepare — generating clause-mapped documentation, running gap analysis, managing audit evidence and keeping you audit-ready between surveillance visits. Book a demo to see it in action.
