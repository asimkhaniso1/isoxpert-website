The challenge is usually the documentation

You have the expertise. The paperwork is the bottleneck.

If you're a NEBOSH-qualified or experienced HSE professional, you already understand risk, controls, and how a management system should work. Clients need that knowledge — but ISO projects live or die on the documentation: the manuals, procedures, registers and records an auditor expects to see.

Building all of that from a blank page is the single biggest time cost in ISO consulting. It's what stops many capable professionals from offering ISO services at all. Compliance360 removes that barrier — AI drafts a compliant, clause-mapped starting point, and you apply your expertise to tailor it to the client.

Common questions

How can a NEBOSH or HSE professional add ISO consulting?

Most HSE professionals already understand ISO 45001, ISO 14001 and integrated management systems. The main barrier to offering ISO consulting is producing the documentation. Compliance360 uses AI to generate clause-mapped policies, procedures, registers and records, so you can deliver ISO projects without spending weeks writing documents from scratch.

What documentation can Compliance360 create?

Policies, procedures, SOPs, forms and records, risk registers, clause-mapped documentation and complete audit-ready management systems — mapped to the standard your client needs.

Which standards does Compliance360 support?

ISO 45001 (occupational health & safety), ISO 14001 (environment), ISO 9001 (quality), integrated management systems and more.

Do I need to be a full-time consultant to use it?

No. Freelancers, consultants, trainers and full-time HSE professionals all use Compliance360 to expand their services and take on more consulting work alongside their existing role.
