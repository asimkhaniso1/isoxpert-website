Overview

What is ISO 42001?

ISO/IEC 42001 is the world's first international standard for an Artificial Intelligence Management System (AIMS). It sets out a risk-based framework for developing, deploying and using AI responsibly — covering governance, AI risk and impact assessments, data quality, transparency, human oversight and the full AI system life cycle.

Certification means an independent certification body has audited your AIMS and confirmed it meets the standard. As enterprise buyers add AI-governance questions to procurement and regulators move quickly — from the EU AI Act to sector guidance worldwide — an ISO 42001 certificate is fast becoming the clearest way to prove your AI is trustworthy.

Because the standard is new, early adopters stand out. Company Certification International is an early mover among certification bodies for ISO 42001 — so you get auditors who know the standard deeply, and a certificate that puts you ahead of your market rather than catching up to it.
