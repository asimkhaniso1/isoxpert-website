FAQ

Common questions

What is Inspect360?

Inspect360 is an AI-powered safety and compliance inspection platform. HSE professionals use it to conduct site inspections from a phone or tablet, capture photo and video evidence, perform risk assessments, record findings and corrective actions, and generate professional inspection reports in seconds.

Is Inspect360 suitable for NEBOSH-qualified professionals?

Yes. Inspect360 is designed for experienced HSE and NEBOSH professionals who want to spend their time improving safety rather than writing lengthy reports. It automates the reporting so you can serve more clients with a more professional output.

Which industries does Inspect360 support?

Construction, manufacturing, oil & gas, logistics, healthcare, hospitality, facilities management and more — any operation that runs safety and compliance inspections across single or multiple sites.

Does Inspect360 align with ISO 45001?

Yes. Inspections, risk assessments and corrective actions can be aligned with ISO 45001, local regulations and industry standards, giving you an auditable digital inspection history for every client.
