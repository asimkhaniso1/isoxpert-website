Overview

What is ISO 45001?

ISO 45001 is the international standard for an Occupational Health & Safety (OH&S) management system. It provides a framework to identify hazards, control risks and prevent work-related injury and ill health — with strong emphasis on worker participation and leadership.

Certification means an independent, accredited body has audited your OH&S system and confirmed it meets the standard. The certificate is recognised globally, is frequently required on tenders and by clients, and replaces the former OHSAS 18001 standard.

Beyond compliance, a certified safety system reduces incidents, downtime and insurance risk — and demonstrates genuine care for your workforce.
