Overview

What is ISO 9001?

ISO 9001 is the world's most widely adopted standard for a Quality Management System (QMS). It gives your organisation a process-based framework for delivering consistent quality, meeting customer requirements and continually improving.

Certification means an independent, accredited body has audited your QMS and confirmed it meets the standard. That certificate is recognised globally and is often a requirement to bid for contracts, win larger customers and join approved-supplier lists.

Built on seven quality management principles — including customer focus, leadership, process approach and continual improvement — ISO 9001 works for manufacturers, exporters and service businesses alike.
